There's many applications for artificial intelligence as technology can be applied to many different sectors and industries. As it is used in the industry of healthcare (medicine) for the dose of drugs, surgical operations and X-ray readings. It is used in computers that play chess and self-driving cars. It can be used in the financial industry as it can detect and flag activity in banking and finance such as unusual the usage of debit card , large account deposits and identify which transactions are likely to be fraudulent, tracking fast and accurate credit points ,in addition to automate manually intense data management tasks. It also helps streamline and ease the trade . Each of these machines must consider the sequels of any action they would take, as each one of these actions will impact the end result. In future artificial intelligence will change every industry, but we have to recognize its limits.
